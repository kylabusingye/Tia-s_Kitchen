About

Meet Tia Kahiigi

I am 19 year old girl who lives in Najjera,Uganda with her parents and sisters. In my free time I like to listen to music,cook, dance and spend time with my friends. Through the course of my learning of Culinary,I have developed a great passion for cooking and its culture.During last winter break,I took the opportunity of my free time to create a cookbook of various dishes to depict that message to teenagers to appreciate culture. I decided it would be a great idea to showcase my dishes on my social media. My friends and other various teenagers loved my recipes and encouraged me to start a restaurant because they were interested in eating my food. I was surprised by the outstanding feedback and decided that my restaurant needs an e-commerce website because it is the best way to sustainably sell my food .I couldn't make the E-commerce website by myslef due to my busy schedule of managing university and budgeting for the ingredients I buy for the selection of recipes from my cookbook that I think my customers would love to eat. If she made this website it could affect my chances of getting a degree.I value my education and culinary arts very much so in order to balance it she hired Kyla Busingye to create this e-commerce website called “Tia’s Kitchen”to make it easier for me to plan orders according to schedule, having balance in my studies and my passion for cooking.
